Weekend Events

Every weekend at Rocky Mountain Vista, guests can enjoy a variety of local activities— from live music and art walks to outdoor adventures and community markets.

This Weekend's Highlights

  • Rocky Ridge Farmers Market (Saturday • 8 AM – 1 PM)
    Browse local produce, baked goods, handmade soaps, and artisan crafts.
  • Creekside Live Music Night (Friday • 7 PM – 10 PM)
    Enjoy acoustic performances from local musicians at the Creekside Coffeehouse.
  • Mountain View Art Walk (Saturday • 4 PM – 8 PM)
    Explore galleries featuring regional painters, photographers, and sculptors.
  • Starlight Cinema on the Green (Saturday • 8:30 PM)
    Free outdoor movie screening—bring blankets and lawn chairs!
  • Riverbend Flea & Craft Fair (Sunday • 9 AM – 2 PM)
    Vintage finds, handmade jewelry, snacks, and rotating local vendors.
  • Pine Valley Trail Tour (Sunday • 10 AM)
    A ranger-led, beginner-friendly guided hike featuring wildlife and scenic viewpoints.
